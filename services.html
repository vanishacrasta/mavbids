Heat Pumps

By using a heat pump to cool your home, you really get two systems in one. During the summer, a heat pump can remove heat from your home in the same manner as an air conditioner. During the winter, though, it can use ambient heat in the air outside in order to warm your home. Contact us today to schedule service on heat pump systems in Kuwait.

Solar Air Conditioner

Why not use the power of the sun in order to cool your home? It may sound counterintuitive, but it is a very practical method of doing so, particularly in climates such as ours. A solar air conditioner is a great way in which to cool your home more efficiently.
